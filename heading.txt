@@HTML Headings
-defined with <h1> to <h6> tags.
-  h1->most important heading. h6->least important heading.
- don't use to make text big or bold. It's a heading!

@@HTML Horizontal Rules(duong gach ngang duoi ten dau de)
- defined with <hr>
<p>This is paragraph.</p>
<hr>
<p>This is paragraph.</p>
<hr>
<p>This is paragraph.</p>
<hr>

@@HTML <head> element
-has nothing todo with HTML headings.

@@HTML <title> element
-defines HTML document's title
-will not displayed in the document, but might be displayed in the brower tab.
